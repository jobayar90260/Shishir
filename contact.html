Frequently Asked Questions

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Yes, we offer many vegetarian options and can adapt most dishes to accommodate allergies or dietary restrictions. Please let us know in advance when making your reservation.

Do you offer catering services?

Yes, we offer catering for special events and parties. Please contact us at least one week in advance to discuss your requirements.

Is there a dress code?

We have a smart casual dress code. While we want you to be comfortable, we ask that guests refrain from wearing athletic wear, shorts, or flip-flops during dinner service.

Do you have private dining options?

Yes, we have a private dining room that can accommodate up to 20 guests. Please contact us for availability and pricing.
