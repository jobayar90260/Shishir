Our Story

The heart and soul behind Spice of Bengal

Our Heritage

Spice of Bengal was born from a passion to share the rich culinary traditions of Bangladesh with the world. Founded in 2010 by Chef Kamal Ahmed, our restaurant brings authentic Bengali flavors prepared with traditional techniques and the finest ingredients.

Chef Kamal grew up in Dhaka, where he learned the art of Bengali cooking from his grandmother. Her secret recipes and techniques have been carefully preserved and form the foundation of our menu today.

What started as a small family restaurant has now grown into a beloved culinary destination, known for its authentic flavors and warm hospitality.
